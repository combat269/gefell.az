Lift-Slide (HBSB) System

  • Load capacity up to 400 kg
  • Smooth and silent sliding mechanism
  • Premium square-shaft handle design

KNG HBSB systems are designed for the comfortable movement of heavy, wide glass sashes (up to 3.3 metres tall). Thanks to special roller sets and corrosion-resistant gearboxes, the system runs smoothly and silently even under maximum load. It maintains high sealing, preserving sound and thermal insulation.

Single Opening Window System

  • European standard C-channel compatibility
  • Multi-point locking
  • Ergonomic handle design
Learn more ▾

KNG's inward-opening-only systems stand out for high static strength and simplicity. Equipped with Master and Master Pro series handles, this system is protected by a special corrosion-resistant coating (oxidation treatment) that has passed a 1000-hour salt spray test. A long-lasting, reliable solution for public buildings and offices.

Double Opening Window System

  • RC2 security level
  • Sash weight up to 130 kg
  • Choice of concealed or visible hinges
Learn more ▾

This system provides both full window opening (180°) and a safe top-vent ventilation mode. KNG's dedicated "Anti-lift" and "Anti-pry" locking elements provide high resistance to forced entry. The system has passed a 100,000-cycle opening-closing test and maintains full sealing even in the harshest climate conditions.

KNG Standard Door Series

  • Multi-point lock body
  • Door hinge load up to 160 kg
  • Adjustable strike plates
Learn more ▾

KNG door systems are designed for both single- and double-sash constructions. Adjustable door hinges prevent the door from sagging over time. The multi-point locking mechanism ensures the door presses fully against the frame at every point, creating maximum wind and sound insulation.

TITAN — Window System

PVC Profile · PVC-channel Aluminium Profile

  • Compatible with PVC and PVC-channel aluminium profiles
  • Burglary protection up to RC2
  • Standard hinged (visible) opening
Learn more ▾

The TITAN window system is designed for both PVC profiles and PVC-channel aluminium profiles. It creates even compression across the full perimeter, maintaining maximum air, water and sound insulation. A standard hinged (visible) opening solution.

The PVC-channel aluminium version provides stable load distribution for larger, heavier sashes, while the PVC version is optimised for standard-sized windows. Both versions are equipped with high-quality steel components and SIEGENIA's dedicated surface-treatment technology.
