Partnership · May 2026

The AXOR team visited us in Baku

In mid-May, an AXOR delegation visited Baku and met with GEFELL MMC. At the meeting, AXOR introduced its new Smart GT HBSB (PVC profile) lift-slide system and its concealed-hinge PVC profile window system.

Read more ▾

In mid-May, an AXOR delegation travelled to Baku to meet with the GEFELL MMC team. Our guests were warmly welcomed in Baku, and the meeting covered the future direction of our partnership.

At the meeting, AXOR introduced its new Smart GT HBSB (PVC profile) lift-slide system, as well as its concealed-hinge PVC profile window system. Both systems are already in GEFELL MMC's warehouse and actively being sold.

These systems come with high-quality accessories and a 10-year official manufacturer warranty.

Partnership · August 2025

GEFELL MMC becomes the official distributor of KNG

GEFELL MMC and KNG have signed a partnership agreement. With this, we begin operating as the official sales and supply partner for KNG systems in Azerbaijan.

Read more ▾

An official partnership agreement has been reached between GEFELL MMC and KNG. KNG is one of the internationally recognised, widely applied manufacturers of accessories for aluminium window and door systems.

KNG products are currently used in more than 70 countries and are especially popular in the Russian market. The brand is known for its certified systems, manufactured to international standards and having passed rigorous laboratory testing.

All KNG accessory systems are supplied with a 10-year official manufacturer warranty and are designed for long-lasting, intensive use.

As part of the partnership, meetings between the two parties were held in Baku, where new technologies, product lines and technical solutions were discussed. Thanks to this agreement, we offer KNG's newest systems at prices adapted to the local market, with official documentation and manufacturer confirmation.

As GEFELL MMC, we reliably provide our customers with premium-quality KNG products that meet high standards, backed by an official warranty.
